Disease Detection By Facial Diagnosis

Using deep transfer learning from face recognition, we achieved over 90% accuracy in diagnosing diseases like beta-thalassemia, hyperthyroidism, Down syndrome, and leprosy from 2D face images. This method outperformed traditional approaches and clinicians, offering a promising, low-cost, and non-invasive way for disease screening with small datasets.
